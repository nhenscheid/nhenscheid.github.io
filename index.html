I am a graduate student in the Program in Applied Mathematics at the University of Arizona. I am currently working as a research associate with prof. Harrison Barrett in the Center for Gamma Ray Imaging. I use information theory, random processes, high-performance parallel simulation, and inverse problems theory to understand the role that emission imaging data plays in personalized medicine.

To read more about my research, visit my research page.
