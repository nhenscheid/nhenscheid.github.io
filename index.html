I am a postdoctoral research associate in the Department of Medical Imaging at the University of Arizona. I received my PhD from the Program in Applied Mathematics under Harrison Barrett. I employ random process and statistical inverse problem theory together with high-performance parallel computation to understand the role that emission imaging data plays in precision mathematical oncology.

To read more about my research, visit my research page.
